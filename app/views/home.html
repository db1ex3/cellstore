Sophisticated Semantic Analysis

Compare or aggregate facts across filings, entities, periods, or sectors. Filter by any combination of explicit dimensions. Easily deal with amendments or duplicated information. Apply custom mappings of synonymous concepts. Use business rules to compute unreported facts or key ratios.

Example — Compare the ratio of R&D cost over Revenues across entities in a given industry sector.

Custom Reports

Create reports the way you need them. Add aggregates, ratios, or external information. Create your own validation or verification rules. Compare across competitors or sectors.

Example — Create a normalized Balance Sheet to compare the latest filings of the S&P500 entities.

Real-Time

Perform truly interactive data analysis. Get answers to even the most sophisticated queries in seconds. Slice and dice, drill down, everything in real-time.

Example — Compute - in real time - the sum of all values reported against the concept us-gaap:Assets, on all 10-K SEC XBRL financial filings, aggregated by fiscal period.

Create Your Own Business Information Repository

SECXBRL.info is a prototype repository built on top of 28.io. You can create your own XBRL-based information repository using the same approach, no programming required, just configure settings. You build your own XBRL taxonomy, submit XBRL instances, and you are in business in just a couple of hours. Or, if you need more flexibility, you can even modify existing application profiles to fit your needs or even create your own application profile.

Example — a regulator can specify that the IFRS taxonomy be used to submit information, specify a few configuration settings, and specify XBRL-based business rules. Your repository can be up and running in hours.
